## 3. Legal Consequences

Unauthorized access to computer systems is **illegal** in most jurisdictions:

### United States
- **Computer Fraud and Abuse Act (CFAA)** - 18 U.S.C. § 1030
- Violations can result in fines and imprisonment up to 20 years

### European Union
- **Directive on Attacks against Information Systems** (2013/40/EU)
- Member state laws implementing the NIS2 Directive

### United Kingdom
- **Computer Misuse Act 1990**
- Unauthorized access: up to 2 years imprisonment
- Unauthorized access with intent: up to 5 years
- Unauthorized modification: up to 10 years

### Other Jurisdictions
Most countries have similar laws criminalizing unauthorized computer access.
